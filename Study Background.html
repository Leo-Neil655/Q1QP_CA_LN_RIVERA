The Importance and Relevance of our Study
Our group, group 8 of section Calcium, have decided to take the matter into our own hands, taking the time to gather data and information regarding the connection between prioritization of a certain subject, the method used, the amount of time allotted for the task, and possible links to sleep. With our data collected, we seek to form conclusions out of a set of analyses of our calculations, along with our proper methods of categorizing and presenting the values in a visual manner, such as graphs and charts, to hopefully find possible recommendations for potential research experiments and investigations in the future that may aid student performance and wellbeing, not just for ninth graders but for high school students in general, without sacrificing essentials, especially sleep, which based on personal experience and observations from our group, is the number one life essential forcefully tampered with for the sake of academic or extracurricular workload.
